About Us

Authentic Nepali Hospitality.

At Samira Homestay, we welcome you into our family. Nestled in the heart of Bandipur, our heritage home blends traditional Newari architecture with warm, personalized service. Here, every stay is a cultural journey—from homemade dal bhat at our dining table to sunrise views over the Himalayas.

A Home, Not Just a Stay

Sleep under hand-carved wooden beams, savor recipes passed down for generations, and share stories with our family. This is Nepali hospitality at its purest.

Insider’s Guide to Bandipur

Let us arrange hidden hikes to Thani Mai Temple, pottery workshops with local artisans, or a hike to Siddha Cave(Gufa).

In the Lap of the Himalayas

Wake up to panoramic mountain views from your window. Our homestay sits nestled in Bandipur's hills, where crisp mountain air and golden sunrises over the Annapurnas greet you each morning.

Premium Hospitality

Handpicked organic linens, locally-sourced mountain cuisine, and personalized trekking advice. We blend rustic charm with thoughtful service for an unforgettable mountain retreat.
